//CSS Units 1. Absolute Units These units are fixed and do not change based on the user's settings. Common absolute units include: px (pixels): A fixed unit that is most commonly used. pt (points): Traditionally used in print media, where 1 point equals 1/72 of an inch. cm (centimeters): Used for print layouts. mm (millimeters): Also used for print layouts. in (inches): 1 inch equals 2.54 centimeters.// Absolute Units Example
