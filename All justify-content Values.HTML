Explanation of justify-content Values: flex-start: Items are aligned at the start (default behavior). flex-end: Items are aligned to the end of the container. center: Items are centered along the main axis. space-between: Items are evenly distributed with the first item at the start and the last item at the end. space-around: Items are evenly distributed with equal space around them, but the space between the items is twice as much as the space at the edges. space-evenly: Items are distributed so that the space between any two items, as well as the space before the first and after the last item, is the same. This setup allows you to see how the justify-content property affects the layout of flex items in different ways.
